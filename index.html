Thank you for an amazing journey.

AwesomeWeb was founded in 2013 as a premium, hand-vetted freelance marketplace with one simple mission: to empower freelancers to make the web more awesome.

In a world where big platforms took 30-50% of your hard-earned money, we offered something different—a low, flat subscription fee, no hidden charges, and the freedom to set your own rates and work how you wanted.

Built by freelancers for freelancers.

It worked—for a time. We grew into a community of thousands of talented freelancers and clients, with millions of dollars in confirmed projects. But after 8 years of learning, growing, and connecting with so many amazing people, AwesomeWeb’s journey came to a close in 2021.

We’re incredibly grateful to everyone who partnered with us along the way. You made this possible.
