AwesomeWeb launched in 2013 as a premium, hand-vetted freelance marketplace.

In a world where the big marketplaces were taking 30, 40, and 50%, we had no hidden fees, no percentage of project cuts. Just a small monthly subscription, charge your full rate, and work with your clients however you like.

Built by freelancers for freelancers. One project paid for your account for life.

It worked! For awhile. We had thousands of freelancers and clients with millions of dollars in confirmed projects.

After eight years of getting to meet and interact with some of the world's most talented people, AwesomeWeb ran its course and we shut down in 2021.

Thank you to everyone who partnered with us over the years.
